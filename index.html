FAQ

Wen launch?

Sunday, February 18th 5pm PST

How do I get PARTY at launch?

PARTY will be available to buy on 9mm on PulseChain and Uniswap on Ethereum. Contract address will be the same on both chains and announced at launch.

How do I claim my airdrop?

After the launch of Pool Party, you will be able to claim your airdrop on the Pool Party dapp.

Will Pool Party have admin keys?

No, Pool Party will be a fully trustless, immutable smart contract.

Was there a sacrifice phase?

No. Pool Party will launch as a complete product and will have a liquidity pool launch and airdrop.

How do I claim my PARTY rewards?

By simply holding PARTY liquid, you are eligible to claim HEX, COM and LP token rewards claimable on the Pool Party dapp every 2 weeks.
